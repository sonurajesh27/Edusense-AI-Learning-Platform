# 🤖 Sign2Talk User Guide

## Welcome to Sign2Talk!

Your AI-powered sign language learning companion. Chat with our bot using ASL signs, virtual keyboard, or quick buttons!

---

## 🚀 Quick Start

### 1. Open Sign2Talk
Navigate to the Sign2Talk section in your EduSense app.

### 2. Allow Camera Access
The app needs your camera to detect sign language gestures.

### 3. Wait for AI to Load
The AI model will load automatically (takes 5-10 seconds).

### 4. Start Signing!
Click "Start Detection" and show ASL signs to the camera.

---

## 📖 How to Use

### Method 1: Camera Sign Detection 📷

**Step-by-step:**
1. Click **"▶️ Start Detection"** button
2. Show an ASL sign to your camera
3. Hold the sign steady for 3 seconds
4. Watch the detection overlay show your sign
5. The sign auto-sends to the bot
6. Bot responds with encouragement!

**Tips:**
- Ensure good lighting
- Keep hand in camera frame
- Hold signs steady
- Practice makes perfect!

### Method 2: Virtual Keyboard ⌨️

**For manual spelling:**
1. Find the "Virtual Sign Keyboard" section
2. Click letters to spell words: A, B, C, etc.
3. Click "Space" to add spaces
4. Each letter click sends to bot immediately
5. Build your message in the message builder

**Keyboard Features:**
- A-Z letters in 3 rows
- Space bar
- Show/Hide toggle
- Click sound feedback

### Method 3: Quick Signs ⚡

**For common phrases:**
- **👋 Hello** - Greet the bot
- **🙏 Thanks** - Express gratitude
- **🆘 Help** - Ask for assistance
- **👍 Yes** - Confirm
- **👎 No** - Decline
- **🤲 Please** - Polite request
- **❤️ Love** - Show affection
- **👏 Good** - Positive feedback

One click = instant bot response!

## 💬 Message Builder

   - ⌫ **Delete**: Remove last character
   - 🗑️ **Clear**: Clear entire message
3. **Send**: Click "📤 Send Message" when ready
4. **Bot Reply**: Get intelligent response to full message

## 📊 Statistics Explained

### 🔵 Signs Detected
Total number of signs you've made that the AI recognized.

### 🟣 Messages

### 🟢 Accuracy
Current detection confidence (0-100%).
- **90-100%**: Excellent! Perfect sign
- **75-89%**: Good detection
- **60-74%**: Acceptable
- **Below 60%**: Try again

## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Best Detection:
1. ✅ **Good lighting** - Face a window or lamp
2. ✅ **Clear background** - Plain wall works best
3. ✅ **Center your hand** - Keep in frame
4. ✅ **Steady hands** - Hold sign for 3 seconds
5. ✅ **One hand** - Most ASL letters use one hand
6. ✅ **Practice** - Muscle memory improves accuracy

### For Better Learning:
1. 📚 Start with quick signs (Hello, Thanks, Yes, No)
2. 🔤 Practice alphabet A-Z in order
3. 📝 Build simple words (CAT, DOG, HI)
4. 💪 Challenge yourself with longer words
5. 🎯 Try to reach 90%+ accuracy
6. 🏆 Set daily goals (20 signs, 50 messages)

### Troubleshooting:
- **Sign not detected?** → Hold longer, improve lighting
- **Wrong sign shown?** → Adjust hand position
- **Camera not working?** → Check browser permissions
- **Bot not responding?** → Refresh the page
- **Keyboard not working?** → Click "Show Virtual Keyboard"

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Do I need to know ASL?**  
A: No! Start with the virtual keyboard and quick signs to learn.

**Q: Will my data be saved?**  
A: Currently, chat history resets on page refresh.

**Q: Can I use both hands?**  
A: The AI detects one hand at a time for best accuracy.

**Q: What if detection is wrong?**  
A: Use the virtual keyboard as backup, or try the sign again.

**Q: Is this real ASL?**  
A: Yes! We use authentic ASL fingerspelling (A-Z).

**Q: Can I practice offline?**  
A: No, the AI model requires internet to load.

**Q: How do I improve accuracy?**  
A: Better lighting + steady hands + practice = higher accuracy!
